Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and the sea. It
is often add with depth and
stabiity. It symbolised trust, loyality,
confidence, intelliegence, faith,
truth and heaven

Green

Green is the color of nature. It
symbolises growth, harmony,
freshness and fertility. Green has
strong emotional correspondence
with safety.

Red

Red is the color of blood and fire, so
it is associated with energy, war
danger,strength, power,
determination as well as position
desire and love
